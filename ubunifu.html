How we work.

Admissions

After online appreciation, Our
admissions team will contact you to schedule a personal interview so
that we can make sure we can help
you meet your goals.

Method Of teaching

We use project based Learning to deliver our curriculum. Students and
the tutor will identify a unique
problem and learn by building or working on it.

Classes

All our classes run for 4 hours a day
(9am- 1pm) except robotics that
runs full time (9am - 5pm). Virtual
classes' short courses run for 2 hours a day.

How do we measure success?

Ubunifu College is not a coding

build a product and launch to
market within a given timeline.
